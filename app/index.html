the teacher

My name is Magda Szczepaniak and I have a great passion for teaching my native language, Polish! I hold a Master degree in Polish Literature and Grammar from the University of Wroclaw (2003) and have extensive experience teaching Polish as a foreign language.
Since moving to Australia in 2011, I have taught Polish privately to dozens students from Australia and New Zealand and was also worked at the Brisbane Polish School in 2012.
All my students are different and I love private teaching, where I can help students reach their potential by tailoring lessons to suit their individual needs and learning styles.
I am passionate about Polish language, literature and culture. As a mother, I am also very interested in bilingualism and raising children who speak two languages.
Come and join one of my courses and see how much fun and rewarding learning Polish can be!
